Get Started in 60 Seconds

1

Install

Open Terminal and run:

brew tap Harry-kp/tap && brew install --cask afk

First launch: Right-click → Open → Open

2

Start a Session

Click the AFK icon in your menu bar and select "Start Session". Or open the app and hit the golden button.

3

Take Your Breaks

When time's up, a gentle fullscreen reminder appears. Look away from the screen. Blink. Stretch. That's it.

4

Customize (Optional)

Adjust session length, break duration, chime sounds, and more in Settings. Make it yours.
